INVISIBLE WOMAN

About Invisible Woman

She is Susan Storm. She's a Marvel superhero who has the powers of Invisiblity and Force fields, which she got from exposure to a cosmic storm. She's a member of The Fantastic Four and The Avengers.

Her Superpowers

  1. She can become fully invisible
  2. She can make you invisible too :)
  3. She can move stuff with her mind
  4. She's a super hot badass
  5. She's the most powerful member of the fantastic 4
